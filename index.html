Nigeria

Nigeria is my home country, located in West Africa. It is widely recognized for its rich history, vibrant traditions, and cultural diversity. With over 250 ethnic groups and hundreds of languages, Nigeria stands out as a land full of unique customs, festivals, and artistic expressions that reflect its unity in diversity. Proudly known as the "Giant of Africa," Nigeria also leads the way in many areas such as music, film, sports, and the growing tech industry. This blend of cultural strength and innovation makes Nigeria not only a powerhouse on the continent but also a country with a strong influence on the global stage.
